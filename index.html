Elon Musk

The Architect of Tomorrow

Here is a timeline of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1971 - Elon Musk is born in Pretoria, South Africa to Maye Musk, a model, and Errol Musk, an electromechanical engineer, pilot, and sailor.
  • 1980 - Musk's early interests in computing and entrepreneurship emerge. He teaches himself computer programming and sells his first software, a game called Blastar, at the age of 12.
  • 1995 - Musk attends Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario, Canada, and later transfers to the University of Pennsylvania, where he earns dual bachelor's degrees in physics and economics. He also co-founds Zip2 Corporation, a provider of internet-based city guides, alongside his brother, Kimbal Musk.
  • 1999 - Co-founds X.com, an online payment company that later becomes PayPal after a merger. Zip2 Corporation is sold to Compaq for nearly $300 million.
